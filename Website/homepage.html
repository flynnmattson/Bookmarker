Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

I'm not familiar with Google Extensions, how can I get Bookmarker running? You can easily download the extension from our website's homepage. The extension is also available from Google Chrome's collection of extensions. What is a live feed? A live feed is a place for you to share and see the latest and hottest links. The live feed is meant to filled with current and interesting links users would like to share with everyone.

Account Management

How can I manage my account settings from within the extension? You can manage your account by clicking on the settings button at the bottom of the home screen. You will be redirected to the Bookmarker.com website, where you can manage your account.

Editing Bookmarks

How can I delete multiple links at one time? To delete multiple links, simply select the links to be deleted by checking the corresponding boxes and click on the trash can at the bottom of the page. How can I edit a bookmark? Editing bookmarks is easy with Bookmarker. Simply click on the edit button (insert small button image?) corresponding to the bookmark you want to edit.
